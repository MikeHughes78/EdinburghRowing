For 160 years, Edinburgh University Boat Club has been at the heart of rowing in Scotland, shaping generations of athletes on the Union Canal and beyond. From its earliest crews to today's high-performance programme, the club has combined tradition with ambition. Across changing eras, facilities, and formats, one constant remains: the remarkable Edinburgh student and their commitment to excellence, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of speed. As we mark this milestone, we celebrate the people, stories, and shared standards that continue to define EUBC.

Edinburgh160 is our initiative to create a comprehensive understanding of our history and to reconnect with former students and coaches. Whether you graduated in 2025, or your great grandparent did in 1925 — we'd love to hear from you. We're keen to learn of photographs, film, ephemera and memorabilia; and to hear stories about life at EUBC.
